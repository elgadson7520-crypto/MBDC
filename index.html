📄 Consent Agreement

Metropolitan Better Living Center, Inc. — Informed Consent for Adult Day Program Services


By signing below, I voluntarily consent to the enrollment and participation of the above-named individual in the Adult Day Program operated by Metropolitan Better Living Center, Inc. I understand and agree to the following:


1. Services Provided: The center provides supervised group and individual activities, personal care assistance, meals and snacks, health monitoring, social services, therapeutic activities, and transportation (if applicable).


2. Hours of Operation: The program operates during designated hours. Participants must be dropped off and picked up within scheduled times.


3. Care Plan: An individualized care plan will be developed based on the participant's needs, preferences, and abilities. The care plan will be reviewed and updated periodically.


4. Right to Refuse: The participant retains the right to refuse any service, treatment, or activity offered by the center.


5. Behavior Policy: The center maintains a safe environment. Behavior that endangers the participant or others may result in suspension or discharge from the program.


6. Personal Belongings: The center is not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged personal items. Participants are discouraged from bringing valuables.


7. Communication: Staff will communicate with the primary caregiver regarding the participant's well-being, incidents, and any changes in condition.


8. Fees: Program fees are outlined in the Participant Agreement. Payment is due according to the agreed-upon schedule.


9. Discharge: Either party may terminate participation with appropriate notice as outlined in the Participant Agreement.
